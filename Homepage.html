Hello, I'm Darren Zemanek, and This is my Portfolio!

I am utilizing this website as an easy way to gather and present my creative works.

The works I've gathered range from conceptual game design, to rendered videos of environmental design, to video games that I've created. I have divided them up in specific categories to see the work that I did during projects/classes I attended.

To start viewing my work, go to the drop-down menu labeled "Projects". There's also a model viewer you can use to view specific models in a lit 3D space, found in the "Model Viewer" tab.

If you want to learn more about me or contact me yourself, you can read more in the "About/Contact" tab, where I've also provided some prefered forms of contact.

Thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio website. It means a lot to me that you would explore through my work, as it allows me to not only display my many peices, but to also share a part of my time and self with others.

Thank you, and I hope you enjoy!
