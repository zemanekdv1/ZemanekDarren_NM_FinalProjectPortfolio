Hello!

My name is Darren Zemanek. I'm a 3D artist, environment designer, and game designer who is passionate about crafting fun, immersive visual experiences. I focus more on creating models and enviornments for my experiences, but have also focused on level design and game development.

I spent most of my time in college at Texas A&M University in the Visualization major. I've spent multiple semesters working on multiple types of projects, with artstyles ranging from realistic to stylized. In this portfolio I have some handpicked examples of my work and my progress with the tools and skills over time.

Skills

  • 3D Modeling (Blender, Maya)
  • Texturing (Substance Painter, Photoshop)
  • In-Engine development (Unreal 5, Unity, Godot)
  • Environment design and Layout
  • Game and Level Design

If you would like to contact me for questions or comments about me or my work, you can reach me through email or linkedin.
