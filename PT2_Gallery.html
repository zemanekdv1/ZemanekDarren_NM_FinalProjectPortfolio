Production Techniques - Personal Scene

Production Techniques was a summer class I took to learn and understand the basics of 3D modeling, texture creation, and environmental design. The project I am showing consists of an environmental scene worked on by one person, me, with the allotment of less than half of the used assets in the scene being store-bought or made by someone else. To put it simply, the goal was to create an interesting scene while creating most of the models and textures yourself. I utilized Maya and Adobe Substance Painter for the modeling and texture creation during this project, and Unreal Engine for the scene environment space and rendering software..
