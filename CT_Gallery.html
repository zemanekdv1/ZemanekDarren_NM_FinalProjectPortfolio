Cryptid Taxi

Cryptid Taxi was my first real introduction to a group project focused entirely on game production. In this project, my group created characters, music, blueprints, models, textures, environments, and mechanics within the semester's time period. My focus during this project was asset creation, primarily model and textures, as well as environment and level design. Unfortunately, most of my work is not able to be shown as of yet, due to unfortunate file placements. However, once I gain access to my work, I will be adding them to the gallery below.
